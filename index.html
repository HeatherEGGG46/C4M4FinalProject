My BSA High Adventures

In July of 2024, my Venturing Crew and I embarked on our 1st High Adventure, Philmont Scout Ranch. We did a 12 day backpacking trek, 10 of which was spent backpacking. We covered 59 miles and elevations over 12,000 feet!

In August of 2025, we embarked on our 2nd High Adventure, Sea Base St. Thomas Sailing Adventure. This was supposed to be 5 days of sailing, and snorkeling. Hurricane Erin had other plans and our Adventure was cut in half. We very much enjoyed this adventure and we will return!
